About
=====
MyMusic is a free Open Source Decentralized P2P music streaming server.
You can share your music with anyone you like via the internet. 
Just set up the server on your machine with your music and connect to your computer to listen to your songs.

For the Devs
============
MyMusic uses a python WebFramework called Pylons (http://pylonshq.com/).
Any suggestions or contributions are more than welcome.
Feel free to play around with it or create your own. Send me a link of your application. I would love to check
it out.
