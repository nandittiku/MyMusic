About
=====
MyMusic is a free Open Source Decentralized P2P music streaming server.<br>
You can share your music with anyone you like via the internet.<br>
Just set up the server on your machine with your music and connect to your computer to listen to your songs.

Setting-Up
==========
* Register your Application with facebook http://www.facebook.com/developers/apps.php
  MyMusic relies on facebook for authentication.
  Make sure that the information in the setup files are correct.
  If you are in localhost, you are the admin. You can get your IP on your admin page at http://www.localhost:5000/admin/ (by default)

* Once your application has been set up with facebook, you need to add
  songs to your music library. Go to the admin page http://www.localhost:5000/admin/ (by default)
  and add your music.
  You can also get information and albumart from Lastfm.

* Thats it! You are now ready to share your music with your friends.
